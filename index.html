Rainbow chard has colorful stems. It is very nutritious and is great sauteed or braised.
Garlic is loved around the world for the savory, flavorful punch it gives to a dish.
Eggplant are tasty roasted or fried, and add a pretty purple pop to dishes.
Carrots come in a variety of colors. They are delicious raw, roasted, or added to soups.
Tomatoes are so versatile and add a sweet and acidic flavor to so many dishes - even plain!
Strawberries are a sweet treat eaten fresh, in smoothies, ice cream, or baked goods.
Lettuce comes in so many varieties - all are delicious in salad or for adding fresh crispness to a sandwich.
Shishito peppers make a great sizzling appetizer. Careful though - about 1 in 10 is spicy!
